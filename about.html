Our Vision

SRPCASE strives to emerge as a premiere institute national standards promoting excellence and equity in higher education. To mould the students of the institution through a socially committed, intellectually inclined, value based and culture driven paradigms of learning using the state of the art educational technologies.

Our Mission
To enhance the learning capacity and knowledge of the students by imparting quality education of national and international standards. • To teach students to think critically, objectively, creatively and to be life - long learners, engaged leaders and productive citizens. • To motivate the students to pursue research, to advance knowledge and to address the state, national and global challenges.
Quality policy

SRPCASE strives to impart value based education to improve the knowledge skill and character of our students. We shall ceaselessly engage in improving upon the process as required by needs.

SRP college of nursing

Sri RangaPoopathi College of Nursing was established in the year 2008 under the aegis of Sri Rangapoopathi Educational and Charitable Trust. The College is duly approved by Indian Nursing Council and Affiliated to Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University , Chennai. SRCN has it own Green campus spread over 40 acres in Alampoondi, located strategically in the Chennai to Thiruvannamalai State Highways.
